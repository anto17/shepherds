Pure and Undefiled Religion
Pure and undefiled religion before God and the Father is this: to visit orphans and widows in their trouble, and to keep oneself unspotted from the world - James 1:27
The following homes are either supported or run by SHEPHERDS IN CHRIST INTERNATIONAL. These homes help and provide oppurtunities for growth and education to orphaned children so they acheive their God-given potential and dreams.
The Orphanage homes listed below seek out abandoned kids deprived of care and affection and rehabilitates them by providing a nurturing environment, care, food, shelter and basic schooling through Christian love and compassion.
Your contributions towards this cause go directly (100%) to supporting these Orphanage Homes. At Shepherds In Christ, we thoroughly screen, assess and partner with only such organizations whose primary purpose acheives a truly non profit cause. We continuously assess, evaluate our programs to ensure your giving helps reach and make a positive change in the children's lives in the most efficient manner.
If you are also interested in joining one of our mission visits to India, please write to us. God Bless.
