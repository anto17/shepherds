About Us
Transparency
Precious Partners and Friends,
God has blessed us with the finest Partners and friends in the world! Your faithfulness and financial contributions to Shepherds In Christ International make it possible for us to spread Christian Love to the poor and the needy, the orphans, the elderly and support the Christian Missionaries for the glory of the Lord Jesus.
To help you stay informed, we'd like to take this opportunity to briefly explain our financial accountability.
At Shepherds In Christ International, we are governed by our core set of values which includes and applies to financial integrity. We subscribe to a written statement of faith clearly affirming our commitment to the evangelical Christian doctrine. Advancing the Gospel of Jesus Christ is the singular objective of our financial conduct.
Our Board of Directors is comprised of some dedicated and committed people of God who provide direction for our organization. The Board of Directors receives no compensation and meets to establish policies, approve budgets, and review operational results and ministry accomplishments. All Directors of the Board give financially to Shepherds In Christ International. We hold ourselves accountable to steward your donation dollars in the most effective and efficient manner. Shepherds In Christ International also chooses to undergo a yearly financial audit by an outside accounting firm, in accordance with IRS and industry standards.
All your generous contributions to the ministry are tax deductible to the full extent allowed by law. In order to help our donors determine the tax-deductible portion of their gifts, we receipt all donations showing the fair market value of any goods or services provided to the donor.
Out of respect for individuals' privacy, we do not sell or rent our mailing list to other groups wishing to use the names and addresses of our supporters. We maintain tight security on our list of friends and supporters.
We appreciate your partnership and continued trust. Thank you for all you do!
Sincerely In Christ,
Bro. Sam G Jacobs
